Spitting Distance

Heather Raquel Phillips and I exhibited a two-person installation at Napoleon Gallery (now Pink Noise Projects) in 2019, Spitting Distance. The work was the product of a multi-year collaboration started in 2016. The installation restructured the space, creating several viewing opportunities (a la Étant donnés) through which to engage with the created space populated with sculptural and video elements.
