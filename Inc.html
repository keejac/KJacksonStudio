Incubation Series

The Incubation Series is an interdepartmental collaboration at University of Pennsylvania. Stuart Weitzman School of Design Visual Art graduate candidates host History of Art graduate and doctoral candidates for recurring studio visits. The History of Art participants curate Visual Art participants into exhibitions hosted off-campus.

Kirsten Gill and I ideated the program. Hilary Sanchez Whitham and Haely Chang joined leadership for inaugural 2015-2016 year. Together we organized three exhitibitions: Uncommons at Little Berlin; EchoLocation at Grizzly Grizzly; and Please, Come In at Space 1026. Along with leading the program, Kirsten, Hilary, and Haely participated as inaugural curators. I participated as an inaugural artist.
